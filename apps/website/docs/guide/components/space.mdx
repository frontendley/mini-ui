|direction|间距方向|'vertical' \| 'horizontal' |`horizontal`|
|split|设置分隔符|ReactNode |`-`|
|className|节点类名|string \| string[] |`-`|
|size|尺寸|[SpaceSize](#spacesize) \| [SpaceSize](#spacesize)[] |`small`|
|style|节点样式|CSSProperties |`-`|
